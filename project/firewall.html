Dual-Homed Firewall & DNS Filtering

TOOLS: OPNsense, Zenarmor, Unbound DNS, Wazuh

1. The Objective

The goal was to move beyond standard ISP security by building a custom perimeter firewall. I needed granular control over traffic to simulate an enterprise edge network, including Deep Packet Inspection (DPI) and intrusion detection.

Figure 1: Network Topology


2. Implementation Details

I configured OPNsense on a dedicated appliance with two physical NICs (WAN/LAN). Using Zenarmor, I created policies to block specific application categories (Ads, Trackers, Adult Content). I also integrated Unbound DNS to prevent DNS tunneling attacks.

3. Challenges & Resolutions

Initially, the strict Zenarmor policies blocked legitimate update traffic for my Linux VMs. I analyzed the live logs, identified the false positives, and created specific whitelist exceptions based on the destination IPs.



Impact: Achieved 100% visibility into network traffic and blocked 400+ tracking attempts daily.
